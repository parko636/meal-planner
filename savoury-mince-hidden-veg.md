# Quick Savoury Mince (Loaded with Hidden Veg)

*~25 minutes, serves 4*

## Ingredients

- 500g beef mince
- 1 onion, finely diced
- 1 carrot, grated
- 1 zucchini, grated
- 1 potato, grated
- 1 cup mushrooms, finely diced
- ¼ head broccoli — florets grated, stalk finely diced
- 1 cup frozen peas
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 cup beef bone broth (or dilute to ¾ cup broth + splash of water if too rich)
- 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tbsp plain flour (optional — the grated potato will help thicken naturally)
- Salt & pepper to taste

## Method

Brown the mince in a large pan over medium-high heat, breaking it up as it cooks. Once browned, push it to one side and cook the onion and mushrooms for a couple of minutes until softened.

Stir in the grated carrot, zucchini, potato, and broccoli. Cook for another minute or two, stirring through — they'll start to soften and disappear into the mince almost immediately.

Add the tomato paste (and flour if using), stir through for about a minute, then pour in the bone broth and Worcestershire sauce. Give it a good stir, bring to a simmer, and let it bubble away for 10–15 minutes until it thickens into a nice gravy consistency. The grated potato will break down and help thicken everything up beautifully. The bone broth adds a rich, silky texture to the gravy.

Toss in the frozen peas for the last 3–4 minutes.

Season to taste and serve.

## Serving Ideas

- On toast
- Over mashed potato
- With rice
- In a wrap or toasted sandwich
- Over pasta
