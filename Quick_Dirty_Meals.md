# Quick & Dirty Meals

Minimal-effort backup meals for nights when proper cooking isn't happening. These aren't part of the regular meal plan — they're insurance. Each weekly plan will suggest 1–2 of these and include their ingredients in the grocery list.

## The List

### Eggs on Toast
- **What:** Scrambled or fried eggs on buttered toast
- **Key ingredients:** Eggs, bread, butter
- **Time:** 5 min

### Plain Pasta
- **What:** Pasta with butter and parmesan
- **Key ingredients:** Pasta, butter, parmesan
- **Time:** 15 min

### French Toast
- **What:** Plain french toast
- **Key ingredients:** Bread, eggs, milk
- **Time:** 15 min
