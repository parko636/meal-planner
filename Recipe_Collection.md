## 🆕 Tried & Liked

### Braised Beef Short Ribs with Damper
- **Effort:** 🕐 Weekend Project
- **Time:** 25 min hands-on / 4–4.5 hrs total | **Start by:** 12:15 PM
- **Serve with:** Damper, rice or mash, peas
- **Key flavours:** Smoky, rich, tomatoey, beefy
- **Notes:** Gozney Dome on wood for smoke. No wine — bone broth + tomatoes + Worcestershire. Can fall back to kitchen oven. Damper baked in Dome separately. Bones remove cleanly before serving.
- **Kid verdict:** ✅ Loves it
- **Freezer friendly:** Yes (4–6 extra portions)
- **Full recipe:** Yes

### Beef Stroganoff
- **Effort:** 🍳 Standard
- **Time:** 20 min hands-on / 30 min total | **Start by:** 5:00 PM
- **Serve with:** Penne, egg noodles, rice, or mash + peas
- **Key flavours:** Creamy, savoury, mushroomy
- **Notes:** Scotch fillet only — quick sear is everything. Grate zucchini in for hidden veg. Dijon is mild once cooked, reduce for extra-cautious toddler nights.
- **Kid verdict:** ✅ Loves it
- **Freezer friendly:** Yes
- **Full recipe:** Yes
