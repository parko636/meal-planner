## Tips
- **Default green:** If in doubt, frozen spinach. Always in the freezer, always eaten.
- **Hidden veg strategy:** Grated carrot, zucchini, potato, and broccoli all disappear into mince and sauces.
- **Retry rejected veg** every month or so — toddler tastes change constantly.
- **Dome roasting** is a good way to use residual heat after a pizza or bread cook.
